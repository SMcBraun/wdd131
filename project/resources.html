Get Involved

Learn how you can support animal shelters across Hawaii through volunteering, donating, fostering, or adopting.

Volunteer

Many shelters rely on volunteers to help clean spaces, walk dogs, socialize cats, organize supplies, and support adoption events.

Donate

Donations help provide food, medical care, bedding, cleaning supplies, and other resources that shelters need every day.

Adopt

Adopting an animal provides a loving home while helping local shelters make space for other animals in need.
